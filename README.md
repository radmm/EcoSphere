# EcoSphere 🌿

**EcoSphere** is a mindful, high-fidelity, and scientifically precise carbon footprint auditor, passive lifestyle logger, and quiet sustainability advisor. Focused on clarity and minimalism, it translates complex environmental data into accessible personal adjustments to support an eco-conscious life.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy: Soft Cream Minimalist

EcoSphere utilizes a tailored **Sleek Cream & Fainted Contrast** theme, designed to reduce visual noise and promote calm, intentional interaction:
* **Background Canvas**: Soft, restful cream and paper-like warm tints (`#FAF8F4`) instead of aggressive high-tech slate backgrounds.
* **Typographic Rigor**: Classical elegant serifs for headlines paired with fainted warm grays and deep off-black text.
* **Fainted Accents**: Soft, fainted tones of blue, green, orange, yellow, and red are utilized sparingly to highlight essential carbon sectors while maintaining a clean, organic aesthetic.

---

## 📊 Core Capabilities

### 1. Multi-Sector Carbon Ledger
Decomposes annual greenhouse gas footprints based on real world consumption habits across four key pillars:
* **Mobility & Travel**: Gauges private vehicle fuel efficiency (electric, standard gasoline, hybrid) alongside regional mass transit and annual aviation hours.
* **Household Utilities**: Models monthly electricity usage, natural gas therms, and specific renewable grid supply adjustments.
* **Dietary Footprint**: Tracks vegetarian, vegan, and omnivorous meal preferences alongside local sourcing ratios.
* **Goods & Packaging**: Evaluates fashion cycles, electronics consumption, and municipal separation rates.

### 2. Active Eco Actions Logger
Allows users to record daily mindful choices, offset computed footprints real-time, and view their compounding progress.

### 3. Personalized AI Advisor ("Sustaina")
Features a conversational climate advisor that automatically ingests the user's computed indicators to offer tailored suggestions in a positive, supportive voice.
